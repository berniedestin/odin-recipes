Cake Mix Cookies

Description

This recipe is delightful, easy to make, and surprisingly modular!

Imgredients

  • 1 Box of Cake Mix [Duncan Hines Double Dark Chocolate Fudge]
  • 1/3 Cup of Oil [Grape Seed Oil]
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Eggs [Large]

  • *1/4 Cup Sugar *Optional for Rolling Dough in Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix First Four Ingredients in Large Bowl
  2. Refrigerate Dough for 30 Minutes
  3. Shape Dough into Walnut Sized Balls
  4. Roll Dough Balls in Bowl of Sugar *
  5. Place on Cookie Sheet lined with Parchment Paper
  6. Bake at 350° for 9-11 Minutes
