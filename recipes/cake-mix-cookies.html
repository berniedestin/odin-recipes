Cake Mix Cookies

Description

This recipe is delightful, easy to make, and surprisingly modular!

Imgredients

  • 1 Box of Cake Mix [Duncan Hines Double Dark Chocolate Fudge]
  • 1/3 Cup of Oil [Grape Seed Oil]
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Eggs [Large]

  • *1/4 Cup Sugar *Optional for Rolling Dough in Sugar
