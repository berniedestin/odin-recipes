Schiacciarlo

Description

This recipe is a perfect comfort food that takes about an hour to prepare from start to finish

Ingredients

  • 1lb of pasta(Shellbows)
  • 24oz jar of tomato basil pasta sauce
  • 8oz package of cream cheese
  • 1lb of Ground Italian Sausage
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Spices(Salt, Black Pepper, Paprika, Cayenne Pepper, Rosemary, Italian Seasoning, Garlic Powder)

Steps

  1. Start boiling water for pasta on high, add pasta when ready(should be around step #5)
  2. Put Pasta Sauce in small saucepan over low heat
  3. Put Sausage in large skillet over medium heat
  4. Once the Sauce is warm, add Spices, Parmesan Chees, and Cream Cheese in chunks. Stir periodically until incorperated.
  5. When the Sausage is cooked add Sauce to large skillet, stir to combine
  6. Add cooked pasta to large skillet
  7. Stir for a few minutes to fully incorperate
